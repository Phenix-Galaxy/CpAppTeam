Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 25, 2023

Effective Date: January 15, 2023

Color SMS ("us", "we", or "our") operates the mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Account

We do not require users to create an account to use our Services. We may use your encrypted phone number as a way to privately collect metadata required by Rich Communication Services (RCS), such as message delivery, read receipts, and typing indicators. We do not store the content of your communications on our servers.

Contact Information

We do not collect contact information by default. You may voluntarily provide your email address when submitting a bug report or contacting support.

Messaging Services

Color SMS requires certain permissions to provide messaging features:

  • READ AND WRITE SMS – Read, write and send SMS, MMS and RCS messages.
  • CAMERA – Capture and send photos or videos through MMS.
  • RECORD AUDIO – Record and send audio messages through MMS.
  • READ PHONE STATE – Improve responsiveness and provide messaging services.
  • READ CONTACTS – Select contacts for sending messages.
  • READ EXTERNAL STORAGE – Select files, photos and videos for MMS messages.

We do not store your messages, personal data, images, videos, or audio files on our servers. We do not sell your personal information. Any information collected is solely used to provide messaging functionality.

We may use your phone number and SIM information to facilitate message sending, receiving, and dual-SIM support. Contact phone numbers are used only to display and select recipients for messaging.

Identifiers and Cookies

We may request information from third-party content providers on your behalf. Requests are made through isolated sessions to prevent long-term tracking or profile creation through cookies or similar technologies.

Log Data

Our servers may automatically collect anonymous log information generated when connecting to our Services. Such data does not include your IP address, phone number, user identity, location, device model, carrier, or other personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use trusted third-party providers to help operate our Services, including:

  • Google Cloud Platform
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Firebase

Analytics providers may collect pseudonymous information about your device, including device brand, model, operating system, installed application packages, and software features. Such information cannot be used by us to identify you personally.

Advertisement

Color SMS displays advertisements. Advertising partners may collect information related to ad impressions, clicks, fraud prevention, and ad personalization.

Information collected by advertising providers may include:

  • Android Advertising ID
  • Device Model
  • Language Settings
  • Android Version
  • Cookies and Pixels
  • Anonymous Identifiers
  • Ad Click Data and Timestamps

Current advertising providers may include Google AdMob, Twitter MoPub, Facebook Audience Network, and Smaato.

Users who do not consent to personalized advertising may still see non-personalized ads.

Privacy Consent Management

In compliance with GDPR and CCPA, users are provided with privacy consent options within the application. Consent may be revoked at any time by clearing app data or reinstalling the application.

Users may also reset their Android Advertising ID or contact advertising providers directly to exercise their privacy rights.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Business Transfers

Security of Data

We take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Children's Policy

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old.

In the European Economic Area (EEA), our Services are not directed toward children under the age of 16. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.
